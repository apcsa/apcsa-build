Think about another real world object: a regular, two-slice toaster. What kinds of behaviors and state capture how a toaster operates?

Starting with behaviors is sometimes easiest. The things that people need to do, or have the toaster do, when they are using it include:

  • insert bread in slot 1 or in slot 2
  • start toasting
  • stop toasting
  • pop up the bread
  • remove the bread from slot 1 or slot 2.

Another behavior concerns how dark the toaster is supposed to make the bread.

  • set the bread darkness indicator
